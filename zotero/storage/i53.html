Abstract

The perturbation equations concerning the gravitational and electromagnetic waves around a Kerr-Newman black hole are separated in the limiting case of a small charge and small angular momentum of the black hole. The connection equations between the ingoing and outgoing parts of the waves are derived. Occurrence of superradiant scattering of gravitational waves off an uncharged rotating black hole is explicitly shown.
